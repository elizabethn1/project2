My Favorite Games

These are a few of my favorite board games and card games.

Master Mind

Objective of the game: if you're a codemaker, you want to create a clever code so that the decoder can't guess what it is. If you're a decoder, you want to guess the codemaker's code without running out of guesses.

I like to play this game because it is challenging and fun. When I am the decoder, it also requires me to be strategic with my guesses!

Check out the video below to learn how to play Master Mind!

UNO

Objective of the game: you want to be the first person to get rid of all of their cards.

As much as I like to play games that require me to really use my brain to think through my moves very carefully, I also like to play games that are chill and have low-stakes, and UNO is one of those games.

Check out the video below to learn how to play UNO!

Blokus

Objective of the game: you want to put all of your pieces on the board without letting other people's pieces prevent/block you from putting all of your pieces down.

This is a game that requires me to be strategic with the moves that I make, which is why I like this game.

Check out the video below to learn how to play Blokus!
